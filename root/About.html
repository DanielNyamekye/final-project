THE METHODIST’S STORY

WE STAND FOR LIVES CHANGED BY CHRIST

Our Church started with a small group of people who used to congregate in a small living room in Rotterdam South. Today they're over 500.They posed inquiries such as, "Imagine a church that prioritizes others." This mindset centered on serving others has not only influenced our identity but also continues to shape us. As we embarked on establishing new branches and reaching variouscommunities across Rotterdam, our mission has remained unwavering.Today, Methodist Church Rotterdam stands as a single church with multiple sites spread throughout Rotterdam. Each location offers a consistent, cohesive experience with music and a shared message delivered by a communicator that is broadcasted to every site.

WHAT WE BELIEVE IN

We believe that church isn't just about a building with a cross. It's about people's relationship with God and each other.
